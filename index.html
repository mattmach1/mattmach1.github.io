Texas Bio

Texas, the second-largest state in the United States, is located in the South-Central region of the country. It's major cities like Houston, Dallas, San Antonio, and Austin (the state capital) are hubs for business, technology, and culture. Texas has a strong economy, with key industries including energy (particularly oil and gas), agriculture, technology, and aerospace. Its diverse population and history make it a vibrant and influential state within the U.S.
